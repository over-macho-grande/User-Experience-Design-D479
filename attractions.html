Attractions in Taniti

Cultural & Historical

Taniti History Museum

Explore the rich history of Taniti's indigenous people, early settlements, and local traditions.

Traditional Architecture

Wander through Taniti City and admire preserved structures showcasing native island design.

Local Art Galleries

Visit galleries featuring handcrafted works, paintings, and island-inspired sculpture by local artists.

Scenic Experiences

Volcano Hike

Take a guided hike to Taniti’s active volcano and enjoy breathtaking views from above.

Helicopter Tour

See the island from the sky with unforgettable aerial tours departing daily from Yellow Leaf Bay.

White Sand Beaches

Relax along the stunning beaches that stretch around the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Nightlife & Entertainment

Island Dance Club

Enjoy music and dancing at the island’s newest nightlife destination in Merriton Landing.

Local Microbrewery

Taste locally brewed craft beer in a relaxing pub environment with ocean views.

Arcade & Bowling

Family-friendly entertainment just a few blocks from Taniti’s central market area.

Coming Soon

9-Hole Golf Course

Opening next year! A scenic course located just south of Taniti City, with sweeping ocean views.
